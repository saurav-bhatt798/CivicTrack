How do I report a civic issue? +

You can report civic issues in several ways:

  • Online: Visit our website and use the "Report Issue" form
  • Mobile App: Download our mobile app (coming soon)
  • Helpline: Call our 24/7 helpline at +1 (555) 123-4567

Provide as much detail as possible including photos, exact location, and description of the issue.

How do I create an account? +

To create an account:

  1. Click on "Register" from the homepage
  2. Fill in your personal details (name, email, phone, city)
  3. Choose a strong password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile with additional information

Registration is free and takes less than 2 minutes!

Can I upload photos with my complaint? +

Yes! Photos are highly recommended as they help authorities understand and resolve issues faster.

  • You can upload up to 5 photos per complaint
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Maximum file size: 5MB per photo
  • Photos should clearly show the issue

Click "Choose Files" when submitting your complaint to add photos.

How do I track my complaint status? +

You can track your complaint in two ways:

  1. Online Tracking: Use the "Track Status" page and enter your tracking ID and email
  2. Dashboard: Log in to your account and view all your complaints in the dashboard

You'll also receive email updates when your complaint status changes.

How long does it take to resolve a complaint? +

Resolution time varies depending on the type and severity of the issue:

  • Road damage/Potholes: 3-7 days
  • Street lighting: 1-3 days
  • Sanitation issues: 1-2 days
  • Water supply problems: 2-5 days
  • Major infrastructure: 1-4 weeks

We'll keep you updated throughout the resolution process.

What types of issues can I report? +

You can report any civic or public infrastructure issues including:

  • Roads and potholes
  • Street lighting problems
  • Sanitation and waste management
  • Water supply issues
  • Electricity problems
  • Public transport issues
  • Park and garden maintenance
  • Traffic signal problems
  • Other public infrastructure issues
Is my personal information safe? +

Yes, your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • All data is encrypted and securely stored
  • We only share necessary information with relevant authorities
  • You control your privacy settings
  • We comply with data protection regulations
  • You can request data deletion at any time

Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

What if I need to update my complaint? +

You can update your complaint details by:

  1. Logging in to your account
  2. Going to your dashboard
  3. Finding the complaint and clicking "Edit"
  4. Making necessary changes
  5. Saving the updates

You can also contact support if you need help updating your complaint.

How do I contact support? +

You can reach our support team through multiple channels:

  • Email: support@civictrack.dev
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM)
  • Contact Form: Use the contact page on our website
  • Response Time: We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours
Can I report anonymously? +

Yes, you can submit complaints anonymously, but we recommend creating an account for several reasons:

  • Track your complaint status
  • Receive updates and notifications
  • View resolution history
  • Access your dashboard

Anonymous complaints can still be tracked using the provided tracking ID.
